Wire Colors:
	White/Red = Power
	Black = Grnd
	Blue = SCL
	Green = SDA

Motor Shield:
	m1 = right wheel
	m2 = left wheel

Pins:
	A4 & A5 (or SDA/SCL) = I2C
	pin 2 = left laser
	pin 3 = center laser
	pin 4 = right laser
	pin 5 = ultrasonic TRIG
	pin 6 = ultrasonic ECHO
	pin 13 = debug LED (builtin)

misc notes:
	PCduino's center of mass ~ pin J12
