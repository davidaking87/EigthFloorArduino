Tape Colors:
	Red tape = battery pack power
	White tape = Misc...
		= battery pack GRND
		= holding wires in place

Wire Colors:
	White/Red = Power
	Black = Grnd
	Blue = SCL
	Green = SDA

Motor Shield:
	m1 = right wheel
	m2 = left wheel

Pins:
	A4 & A5 (or SDA/SCL) = I2C
	pin 1 = front laser (purple)
	pin 2 = left laser (orange)
	pin 3 = right laser (brown)
	pin 4 = < BLANK > (purple, just to hold pin 3 in place)
	pin 5 = ultrasonic TRIG?
	pin 6 = ultrasonic ECHO?
	pin 13 = debug LED (builtin)

misc notes:
	PCduino's center of mass ~ pin J12
